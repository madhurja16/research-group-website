Our Research

We focus on key areas of theoretical and computational chemistry to address challenges in sustainable energy and chemical synthesis.

Heterogeneous Catalysis

Modeling reaction mechanisms on solid surfaces to design efficient catalysts for industrial applications, such as CO2 reduction and ammonia synthesis.

Electrocatalysis & Energy

Investigating electrochemical reactions for energy conversion and storage, including water splitting, fuel cells, and battery interfaces.

Surface & Interfacial Science

Understanding the structure and dynamics of solid-liquid and solid-gas interfaces at the atomic scale using first-principles simulations.

Materials Informatics

Applying machine learning and data-driven approaches to accelerate the discovery and design of novel catalytic materials.
