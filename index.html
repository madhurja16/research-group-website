Our Research

We focus on key areas to create intelligent systems that can perceive, reason, and act in complex environments.

Machine Learning & Perception

Developing novel algorithms for computer vision and deep learning to enable robots to understand their surroundings.

Human-Robot Interaction

Investigating intuitive and safe ways for humans and robots to collaborate, spanning shared autonomy and assistive robotics.

Autonomous Navigation

Designing robust control and planning systems for autonomous agents in dynamic and unstructured environments.

Robot Manipulation & Control

Creating systems that allow robots to physically interact with and manipulate objects with dexterity and precision.
